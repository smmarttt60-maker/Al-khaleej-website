Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Shaped by Precision

Al Khaleej Aluminum & Glass Company is a family-owned enterprise founded by Ch Riaz and Ch Shehriyar Riaz, rooted in the heart of Al Baha, Saudi Arabia.

We specialize in high-quality aluminum fabrication and architectural glass installations for residential, commercial, and industrial clients across the Al Baha region. Every project we undertake reflects our unwavering commitment to quality materials, precise engineering, and timely delivery.

From the simplest window to expansive glass facades, our experienced team brings years of hands-on expertise and a deep understanding of local architectural requirements and climate demands.

What We Do

Our Services

From concept to installation, we deliver complete aluminum and glass solutions tailored to your exact specifications.

Aluminum Doors

Heavy-duty aluminum entrance doors, swing doors, and sliding systems engineered for security, durability, and aesthetic appeal. Available in a range of finishes and configurations.

  • Residential & commercial
  • Powder-coated finishes
  • Custom sizing available

Aluminum Windows

Energy-efficient aluminum window systems including casement, sliding, fixed, and awning types. Designed for Saudi Arabia's climate — heat-resistant profiles with optional thermal breaks.

  • Single & double glazing
  • Weather-sealed profiles
  • Fly mesh options

Aluminum Partitions

Modular aluminum partition systems for offices, showrooms, and commercial interiors. Sleek, functional dividers that can be fully glazed, semi-glazed, or solid panel.

  • Full & partial glazing
  • Demountable systems
  • Fire-rated options

Glass Installations

Architectural glass installations including tempered glass, laminated safety glass, tinted glass, mirrors, and structural glazing for facades, balustrades, and shopfronts.

  • Tempered & laminated
  • Tinted & reflective
  • Frameless systems

Custom Aluminum Solutions

Have a unique architectural challenge? Our team designs and fabricates bespoke aluminum and glass solutions — from curved facades and canopies to custom louvers, sunshades, cladding panels, and structural frames — built precisely to your drawings and specifications.
